Ginfluence Commercial

One of my favorite projects I've worked on this year was a commercial for a fake brand of gin called Ginfluence. The project consisted of three weeks of writing a script, creating a story board, filming and editing a 15 second commercial. While it's not perfect I'm still very happy with the final result and have had a great time learning video production.
